HU Map

Your University Information Hub

HU Map is a comprehensive university map application designed to serve as a one-stop solution for students, faculty, and visitors. It provides a visual representation of the university campus along with key features, including announcements, event updates, and integrated Google Maps for locating university facilities.

Key Features:

  1. Interactive Campus Map:
    • A user-friendly, interactive map that displays the entire university campus.
    • Zoom and pan functionality to explore different areas of the campus.
    • Clearly labeled buildings, facilities, parking lots, and landmarks.
  2. Google Maps Integration:
    • Seamless integration with Google Maps for real-time navigation to and from the university.
    • Turn-by-turn directions for driving, walking, or cycling.
    • Location-based services for finding nearby restaurants, hotels, and public transportation.
  3. Announcements and Updates:
    • A dedicated section for university announcements, news, and updates.
    • Push notifications for important events, class cancellations, and emergency alerts.
    • Integration with university social media accounts for instant access to posts and updates.
  4. Event Calendar:
    • An event calendar showcasing university-related events, lectures, workshops, and sports activities.
    • Event details, including date, time, location, and descriptions.
    • The ability to add events to personal calendars and set reminders.
  5. Campus Facilities Information:
    • Detailed information about university facilities, including academic buildings, libraries, sports centers, and dining areas.
    • Facility hours of operation, contact information, and available services.
    • Ratings and reviews for user-generated feedback.
